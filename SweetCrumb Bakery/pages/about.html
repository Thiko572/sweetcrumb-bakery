About Us

Our story, mission, vision, and passion for baking.

Our Story

SweetCrumb Bakery began in 2025 as a home-based bakery started by Michael Mukwevho. What began with birthday cakes for friends quickly grew into a full-time business and later a retail shop.

Mission

To bring joy through freshly baked, high-quality cakes and biscuits using traditional recipes and local ingredients.

Vision

To become the most loved local bakery known for custom celebration cakes and biscuits.

Target Audience

Families, event planners, working professionals, and gift shoppers looking for fresh baked products.
